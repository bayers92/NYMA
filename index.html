About NYMA

New York Medical Angels (NYMA) is a Upstate New York based seed stage investor group. NYMA exclusively makes investments in the life science and healthcare sector. NYMA members are carefully selected based on knowledge and experience in medical, hospital administration, biopharma and other healthcare related fields. NYMA members are passionate about supporting early stage medical companies and lending their resources and knowledge to help build sustainable businesses.


Bring early staged Life Sciences technologies to the market and clinic through new start-up companies

Create a new Life Sciences industry hub in the Upstate New York area

Realize above average investment returns through careful screening and due diligence

Our Process

We target companies in the Medical/Life Science space with a strong business model, strong team, and defensible IP. We are a new group, but are targeting investments in the $200K range.

We make investments using the following process:

  • Candidate companies must submit their business plan and supporting documentation to our gust.com site.
  • The companies are then screened and companies are recommended to present at an NYMA meeting. Selected companies are notified.
  • Presenting companies will have 20 minutes to present and 10 minutes to answer any questions the members may have. There will be a short internal conversation among members.
  • After the presentation, the members will be polled to see if there is any interest in investing in the company and participating in due diligence. If there is positive feedback on both, we move the company into our due diligence process. We will inform the company on the decision within a week on the presentation.
  • A due diligence committee is formed to answer the questions raised in the meeting.
  • The committee composes a recommendation for the investment. There is no set timeframe for this process, but we will try hard to finish in a timely manner.
  • If there is a positive recommendation, NYMA will collect investment from the members and participate in the financing round. NYMA does not have to lead the round, but requires an experienced lead investor.
